How to read this sheet

p value is the fraction of students who earned full marks on the item — the lower the number, the harder the question. Mean is average points earned. Each option lists how many students selected it and its share; the keyed answer is marked ✦. A distractor no one chose (░) is non-functioning; one chosen by more students than the key (†) is a miskey or a genuinely seductive wrong answer worth review.
